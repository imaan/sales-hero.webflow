What can Sales Hero do for me and my team?

Get more done in less time, delegate tasks and simplify your sales process

CRM Management

Keep reps laser focused on their next most valuable action.

No-touch CRM data entry makes your sales process run 10x smoother, while pre-call battle plans, action item tracking, and CRM-native dashboards, your reps will feel like they’ve unlocked a cheat code.

Prospecting

Boost your sales pipeline with smart prospecting.
‍
Getting new leads and nurturing existing ones takes time, but it doesn’t have to be your reps time. By delegating your outreach activities, our team will fill the top of your funnel while you focus on deals ready to close.
